📅 Ex-Dividend Date — the key date

To earn a dividend you must buy the stock before the Ex-Dividend Date. If you buy on or after that date, the dividend goes to the previous holder.

📌 Ex-Date = 1 day before Record Date
💸 Buy before Ex-Date → get dividend
⚠️ Stocks often dip by dividend amount on Ex-Date
